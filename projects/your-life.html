YourLife

Description

SEPT-DEC 2022

For one of my classes, my teammates and I were tasked to create a website that creates a slideshow-style video about yourself using a few easy questions. We used the Flask API and various image crawling and photo editing python libraries. Targetted towards college and professional settings where the tast of self-introductions can be a chore, YourLife provides a simple way to turn icebreakers into an audio-visual experience.

The user first navigates to our website and answers some questions. Once they are done, they hit the "Submit" button which outputs a generated transcript of their video. If the transcript is acceptable, they will then click "Generate Video" and our backend will compile images related to their answers and edit them together using basic "Ken Burns" style transitions and a voiceover.

Frameworks/Packages

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python (Flask, ffmpeg, moviepy, icrawler, gtts)
